The Government of Nepal, through the Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation and the Department of Tourism, has implemented an Online Trekking License System to streamline the process of issuing and managing trekking licenses. This system offers a convenient way to search for printed cards for trekking guides who have successfully completed the mandatory physical training provided by the Ministry.

Key Features

Easy Access: The Online Trekking License System can be accessed through the official website of the Department of Tourism. This system is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring that trekking guides and other stakeholders can easily navigate and use the platform.

Smart Card Search: Once trekking guides have passed the required physical training, their details are uploaded into the system. Users can search for printed cards using the guide's unique identification number or other relevant details. This feature helps verify the authenticity and status of a trekking guide’s certification.

Centralized Database: All data related to the trekking guides and their certifications are stored in a centralized database managed by the Department of Tourism. This ensures data integrity and provides a single source of truth for all trekking-related certifications.

Efficient Verification: The system allows for quick and efficient verification of trekking guides' credentials. Tourists and tour operators can use the online platform to ensure that the guides they hire are officially certified and meet the necessary training requirements.

Support for Physical Training Records: The system maintains records of all physical training sessions conducted by the Ministry. This includes details of the training programs, participants, and their performance. Such records are crucial for maintaining high standards in the trekking industry.
